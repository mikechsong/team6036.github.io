Our Robot

Regional Competitions

Each year we participate in two regional competitions, San Franciso Regional(SFR) and Sillicon Valley Regional(SVR). We were semifinalists at SFR last year.

About

FRC Team 6036, Peninsula Robotics, was created by a Palo Alto High School alumnus. He created this team when he found that there were an overflowing amount of students at nearby high school teams. Therefore, to alleviate some of this stress, he created this community team with the idea that everyone should have an equal opportunity in STEM and in robotics.
